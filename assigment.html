SHIVA TEMPLES

Shiva is the Supreme Lord. The 12 Jyotirlingas temples of SHIV in India are Somnath, Nageshwar, Bhimashankar, Trimbakeshwar, Grishneshwar, Vaidyanath, Mahakaleshwar, Omkareshwar, Kashi Vishwanath, Kedarnath, Rameshwaram, and Mallikarjuna.

SHAKTI TEMPLES

Shakti is power of Lord Shiv. Shakti is known with many names as Uma, Sati, Parvati, Durga, and Kali .Shakti, the embodiment of power. There are 52 Shakti Peeth Temples.Some of them are Kamakhya, Purneshwari, Vimala, Bhadarkali, Tripurmalini, Jwalaji.
